Create or update a structured task list.

## When to use
- Planning multi-step tasks before execution
- Tracking progress during complex operations
- Organizing sub-tasks for clarity

## Parameters
- `todos`: List of {content, status} objects
  - status: "pending", "in_progress", or "completed"

## Best practices
- Create todos at the start of multi-step work
- Mark items complete as you finish them
- Keep descriptions short and actionable
